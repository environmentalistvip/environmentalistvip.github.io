About

Our mission is to represent the silent majority who care about the environment but do not want to bear the costs of environmental action.

This blog is for you, the silent majority who has no voice:

  • You care about the environment, but you are too important to adjust your living standards or the way you go about your life.
  • You want to project an image of a champion for the environment without putting any effort to actually save the environment.
  • You long to be perceived by your friends as a warrior for the environment without sacrificing anything.
  • You support environmental action only if paid by others.
  • You feel that environmentalism is going too far or does not make sense, when the environmentalism imposes inconvenience for you.
  • You face too much competition from virtue signalers who are better than you and have hard time to stay on top. Let's face it, the competition out there is brutal.
  • You are frequently shamed by more virtuous environmentalists and you lack the ability to defend yourself.

If any of these points resonate with you, join the silent majority and become a master environmentalist in the eyes of your peers.
